# **App Name**: Iron Wolf

## Core Features:

- Daily Workout Overview: Displays the user's scheduled workout for the current day, including exercise types, focus areas, and duration, mirroring the discipline and structure of martial arts training.
- Workout Progress Tracker: Allows users to log completed workouts, track key metrics like completion time or repetitions, and mark sessions as finished to monitor their journey.
- AI-Powered Workout Generator: Provides a generative AI tool that suggests personalized workout routines and specific drills based on the user's skill level, stated goals, available equipment, and past performance.
- Personal Goal Setting: Enables users to set and track their fitness or martial arts training goals, offering clear milestones and progress visualization to maintain motivation.
- User Authentication: Secures user accounts and personalized data, ensuring private access to workout plans and progress history.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant, electric blue (#38BDF8) chosen to reflect the energy, modernity, and focused intensity inherent in martial arts and disciplined training.
- Background color: A very dark, desaturated blue (#0F1619) provides a deep, calming foundation that emphasizes content and mirrors a focused training environment.
- Accent color: A light, crisp aquamarine (#A6F0EA) to highlight interactive elements, notifications, and key information, creating strong contrast against the dark background.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for its modern, technical, and impactful feel, perfect for bold titles and section headers. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its high legibility and neutral, efficient aesthetic, suitable for detailed workout instructions and textual content.
- Utilize bold, minimalist vector icons with a sharp, geometric design, consistently depicting sports, martial arts, and fitness themes, often in the primary blue accent.
- A clean, dark-themed layout that uses well-defined cards and containers to structure information. Generous use of padding and margins for breathability, with key elements centered for emphasis, reflecting a focused and uncluttered training philosophy.
- Subtle and swift animations for state changes (e.g., workout completion, navigation between screens), enhancing responsiveness without distracting from the user's focus on training goals.
